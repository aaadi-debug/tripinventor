About Trip Inventor

We're truely dedicated to make your travel experience best

At Trip Inventor, we're passionate about crafting personalized, authentic, and meaningful travel experiences. Our journey began in 2018, when our founder, Mr. Harpreet Singh, a seasoned travel industry professional, set out to revolutionize the way people experience travel.

Our Notable Milestones

Trip Inventor has achieved the following notable milestones:

  1. Formed strategic partnerships with 23 Destination Management Companies, expanding our global reach and expertise.
  2. Recognized by the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways for our contributions to the travel industry.
  3. Certified by IATA, demonstrating our commitment to industry standards and best practice.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire global understanding and appreciation by designing seamless and stress-free journeys that connect travellers with local cultures. We're committed to delivering exceptional customer experiences, innovative travel solutions, and making a positive impact on the world.

Our Vision

To be a leading travel brand globally, recognized for exceptional customer experiences, innovative travel solutions, and positive impact on the world.

Our Values

At Trip Inventor, we're guided by a set of core values that shape our approach to travel and business:

Authenticity

We remain true to our purpose, vision, and mission, always striving to deliver genuine experiences.

Customer-Centricity

We prioritize our customers' needs, desires, and well-being, tailoring our services to exceed their expectations.

Innovation

We embrace creativity, continuously seeking novel solutions to enhance our offerings and stay ahead of the curve.

Sustainability

We commit to environmentally friendly practices, responsible tourism, and supporting local communities.

Respect

We treat our customers, partners, and team members with dignity, empathy, and kindness.
